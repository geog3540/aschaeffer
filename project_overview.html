Life in Southwest Wisconsin An exploration of Southwest Wisconsin

I recently accepted an offer to work for the Southwest Wisconsin Regional Planning Commission (SWWRPC) in Platteville, Wisconsin. While I've traveled to Wisconsin for family vacations, I haven't spent time in Platteville or any of the communities in the southwest corner of the state. With that in mind, the goal of this project will be to explore southwest Wisconsin. My goal in designing this project is to use maps, statistical displays, and potentially images and other media to tell a story about southwest Wisconsin.

I hope to answer the following questions with this project:

  • What counties are included in SWWRPC's professional area?
  • What is the population in those counties?
  • What are the environmental characteristics of the area (i.e. topography, major rivers or lakes, rainfall, climate)?
  • Are there any environmental hazards to be aware of?
  • What about other recreation areas like state parks or even city parks? Where are those located?
  • Are there any large farming operations in the area (think dairy farms since it's Wisconsin)?
  • What are the communities within SWWRPC's area? How big are the communities?
  • What are other characteristics of the communities(demographics, education, income, etc.)?
  • Are there any community challenges that all of this information alludes to?
