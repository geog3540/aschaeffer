Community Characteristics

Southwest Wisconsin is composed of 52 towns and villages. Platteville, home to the Southwestern Wisconsin Regional Planning Commission, is the largest of the communities with 11,764 people. It is also home to a branch of the University of Wisconsin. The next largest community is Monroe with 10,537 people. Nearly all of the other communities have a population under 5,000 people. For context, the Madison metropolitan area is approximately an hour and a half from Platteville.
