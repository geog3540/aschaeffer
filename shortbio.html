Alyssa Schaeffer Regional Planner

Following my bachelor’s degree in environmental engineering and my participation in service organizations on the University of Iowa campus, I chose to pursue a master’s degree in urban and regional planning to combine my passions for solving environmental issues and social justice issues. I hope to use my degree to collaborate with community members and make neighborhoods and cities more sustainable and inclusive for all. My past work experiences make me especially qualified to work on diverse teams with innovative projects. In 2022, I decided to add a focus in GIS to my master's degree. You'll find examples of my mapping throughout this portfolio.
