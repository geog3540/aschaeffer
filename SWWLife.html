Life in Southwest Wisconsin An exploration of Southwest Wisconsin

I recently accepted an offer to work for the Southwest Wisconsin Regional Planning Commission (SWWRPC) in Platteville, Wisconsin. While I've traveled to Wisconsin for family vacations, I haven't spent time in Platteville or any of the communities in the southwest corner of the state. With that in mind, the goal of this project will be to explore southwest Wisconsin. My goal in designing this project is to use maps, statistical displays, and potentially images and other media to tell a story about southwest Wisconsin.
